Our Escape Rooms

High Speed NYC

    Book Now

You hop on train heading to Central Park for a fun day, but before you know it you realize that the train has been abandoned by its conductor! The train is now moving at high speed without a driver. The horror! As the clock ticks down, you must figure out how to activate the emergency systems and stop the train before it's too late!

High Speed NYC is an exciting, innovative, and advanced high-tech game guaranteed to give you an escape experience like no other!

Minimum players- 2

Recommended max players- 8

Recommended for ages 9+

Difficulty level- 7.5

Sugar Rush

    Book Now

Your team enters the great NYC cupcake baking challenge, but when you arrive at the state-of-the-art bakery you notice that there are no ingredients in sight... and the door is locked!! You realize that you have only one hour to somehow create and present to the judges a set of delicious cupcakes.

Sugar Rush is our newest escape game with a culinary twist that will put all your senses to work!

Minimum players- 2

Recommended max players- 8

Recommended for ages 9+

Difficulty level- 7.5

Operation Dive

    Book Now

The USS Growler submarine has been decommissioned since 1964, but with the threat of a new attack on New York City from a hostile enemy submarine carrying a destructive missile, the Pentagon has ordered an immediate counter-operation. You and your team were ordered to re-activate the Growler, submerge her and carry out "Operation Dive".

Operation Dive is our newest high-tech filled escape room- an immersive and unforgettable experience!
