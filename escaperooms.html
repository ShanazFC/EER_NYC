Exit Escape Room NYC is the highest rated escape room venue featuring the most immersive, interactive and thrilling escape games in New York City. Our escape rooms were expertly designed to provide the ultimate fun challenge fit for any age group.

Our Escape Rooms

High Speed NYC

You hop on train heading to Central Park for a fun day, but before you know it you realize that the train has been abandoned by its conductor! The train is now moving at high speed without a driver. You must figure out how to activate the emergency systems and stop the train before it's too late!

High Speed NYC is an advanced high-tech game guaranteed to give you an escape experience like no other!

Sugar Rush

You and your teammates have entered the great NYC cupcake baking challenge, however once you enter the state-of-the-art bakery you notice that there are no ingredients in sight... and the door is locked!! As your timer starts you realize that you have only one hour to somehow create and present the judges with a set of delicious cupcakes. But how? Sugar Rush is our newest and most fun, interactive and exciting escape game, with a culinary twist, that will put all your senses to work! Oh, and should you successfully complete the challenge on time... you'll get to enjoy your masterpiece cupcake creations... Bon-apetite!

Operation Dive

The USS Growler submarine has been decommissioned since 1964, but with the the sudden rise of a new attack threat to New York City from a hostile enemy submarine carrying a destructive missile due to be deployed in one hour, the Pentagon has ordered an immediate counter-operation. You and your team were ordered to re-activate the Growler, submerge her and carry out "Operation Dive". Can you stop the attack and save the city before time expires? Operation Dive is our newest high-tech filled escape room- an immersive and unforgettable experience!

The Mission

It has been revealed that there is a double agent in the international intelligence agency IIA. You and your team were selected to take on a top priority mission and expose the double agent. In order to complete your mission you will need to follow a trail of clues. Attention to detail and team work will help you along the way. The mission is a thrilling escape game that will put your detective skills to test​.
